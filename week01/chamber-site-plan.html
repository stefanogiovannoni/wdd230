Overview

Purpose

The Palermo Chamber of Commerce serves the city as the main host and leader in promoting economic growth. We help communities to strenghen relationships to improve our local economies and our way of living by hosting presentations alongside with networking events

Audience

Business Owners, Entrepeneurs, Investors & Philantropists
